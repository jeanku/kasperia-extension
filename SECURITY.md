---

## Responsible Disclosure Guidelines

When reporting a vulnerability, please include:
1. A detailed description of the issue.
2. Steps to reproduce the vulnerability (if applicable).
3. Any relevant logs, screenshots, or proof-of-concept code.

Please:
- Do **not** publicly disclose the vulnerability until it has been resolved.
- Do **not** attempt to access user data or exploit the issue beyond testing.
- Respect user privacy and data integrity.

## Bug Bounty

We currently do not have a formal bug bounty program,  
but we greatly appreciate and recognize responsible disclosures.  
Significant findings may receive public acknowledgment or future rewards.
